What are Dynamic Product Ads?

Creatives labeled "Dynamic Product Ad (Catalog)" use Meta's Dynamic Product Ads (DPA) feature. The template variable {{product.name}} is automatically replaced with actual product names from your catalog when shown to users. These ads are highly effective for retargeting and typically show the highest CTR (up to 4.13% in your data).
